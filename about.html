About

My Story

I’m an educator turned UX Designer with an affinity for research I enjoy thinking about different methods to answer research questions that solve problems. By combining the theory grounded world of academic research with the dynamic methods of user research, I create user centred and learnable designs.

After working in international higher education management for 10+ years, I transitioned into UX design because I believe in the need for both qualitative and quantitative user research in understanding online learning trends. I am passionate about acknowledging users’ needs and frustrations as the foundation to improving functionality, ease of use and enjoyment.
